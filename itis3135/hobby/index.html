Who collects Pokemon Cards?

Nowadays, its not only just kids that collect these shiny pieces of cardboard, but also adults!

From people that used to collect cards back when it first came out in the late 90's, kids that grew up watching the show, and parents passing on their love of Pokemon to their kids!

Now, lets dive into the many type of people that collect Pokemon Cards and why they do it:

Collector Incentive
Casual Collect for the fun of it, nostalgia, or because Pokemon is special to them
Competitive Collect to build playable decks to battle other players in matches/tournaments
Investor Collect cards with the idea they will go up in value over time and eventually sell
Master Set'er Collect every card in a set or series to have a complete collection
Vendor Collect to resell cards/sealed product for profit and use the profit to get more cards

Where to collect Pokemon Cards?

There are many places to get Pokemon Cards nowadays, although many desirable sets may be difficult to find because of the recent hype in the hobby.

These places include:

  • Local Card Shops
  • Big Box Retailers (Walmart, Target, etc.)
  • Online Marketplaces (eBay, TCGPlayer, Facebook Marketplace etc.)
  • Card Shows
  • Trading with others

However, it is important to take notes of fakes when buying online or trading with others, and do your research!
